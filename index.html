about eclipse

Eclipse Bar & Grill is the area’s newest and best establishment for good food, good drinks, and good vibes. Located right on Main Street, Eclipse is the perfect place for a quick bite or a night out. Whether you’re getting some fresh air on our rooftop bar or eating a gourmet meal, Eclipse has something for everyone!

Eclipse was founded in 2021 by two best friends with a shared passion of making an impact on the local community and serving great meals. We always use fresh, wholesome ingredients in our food, many of which are sourced locally. Come see what we're all about!
